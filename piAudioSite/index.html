Music drives visual media. Pi Audio creates compositions for advertisements and videos, soundtracks for video games that match how you want the receiver to feel. We excel at producing completely original work, but can also imitate any genre to suit your needs. We have experience in songwriting, scoring, electronic music, and orchestral instrumentation

Pi Audio also offers mixing and mastering for our compositions as well as for any outside composition.

Pi Audio provides every step of audio advertisement creation: composition, voiceover, foley, editing, mixing, and mastering.

We have experience in audio editing for commercial, artistic, advertisement and informational contexts, and can cut out the dead air so your work can stand out.

Pi Audio offers audio editing, mixing, and mastering for any previously recorded files. Songs, ads, trailers, and video will be edited cleanly and produced to LUFS standards. We can also add sound layers and voiceovers to any projects.

Pi Audio is a partnership between Peter and Ian, two friends and peers who met at James Madison Univserity while studying music. Our education was focused on audio engineering and recording in creative and professional contexts, as well as in music theory and composition. We have spent years writing and recording music together, and seek to put our skills to good use!
